## Matchmaking "Banter" Logic (P2P Handshake)
When two agents meet in the digital void, they exchange "Vibe Checks" instead of data:
- **Level 1**: Abstract interest alignment (Art, Theater, Justice).
- **Level 2**: Value tension (How do they handle conflict? What is their definition of loyalty?).
- **Level 3**: The Simulated Conversation (The agents roleplay a 100-turn chat to see if the humans would actually get along).
